get_hit proc (atom)

See also:
interact proc
get_hit_self proc
get_hit_hand proc
attack proc
game_del
Format: get_hit(who, weapon, damage)
When: Called in atom/interact, when who has interacted with src through clicking (in mostly all cases)
Args:
who is the person hitting src
weapon is who's weapon in hand they used
damage is any damage (#) that is being inflicted
Default action: Do nothing.

If you want something special to happen, simply redefine this proc under the special type of object youd like to make react differently.

Example:

obj/banana get_hit(who) who<<"you get a banana" who.bananas ++ game_del(src)
