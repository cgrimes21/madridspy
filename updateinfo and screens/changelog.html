Version 0.76;

  • - Fixed: Ritter Intelligence Service entrance door should be fixed.
  • - Fixed: Id cards from allies can't be traded in for cash.
  • - Fixed: Lockpicks should now be functioning, and spawn in your vendor.
  • - Fixed: Viewing through a door's keyhole should now work in all instances.

  • - Changed: The penalty for killing allies has been greatly increased. Guys c'mon..
  • - Changed: The sniper's range has been doubled to 24 tiles in addition to scope ability.
  • - Changed: The sniper has a minimum range of 6 tiles, the weapon can't be used for close-range combat.
  • - Changed: Stun has been slightly nerfed - you can now use objects while stunned.
  • - Changed: Grab has been slightly nerfed with a small cooldown.
  • - Changed: Increased the price of a few items.

  • - Added: The sniper now has a scope ability. It appears as a verb in your command panel.
  • - Added: Bombs can be thrown, but are almost instant. Be close to your target.
  • - Added: Flash bombs will blind for ~5 seconds except the user. Thrown like a smokebomb.
  • - Added: Thermal helmets can be worn in the head slot, they see spies through walls.
  • - Added: Helmets have been added to protect from bludgeoning (stunning/ko-s)

  • - Note: Mind your cash now, some items are expensive. Hopefully the need for cash will increase the urge to assassinate.
